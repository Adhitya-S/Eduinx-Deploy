How the Engine
Works.

A transparent look at the computational layers that transform unstructured resumes into verified hiring signals.

Engine Inputs: JDs Resumes Skill Rubrics Interview Notes

How it works: Three steps only.

1. Ingest

Mandates + resumes are structured.

2. Score

Execution checkpoints are validated.

3. Route

Signal-rich candidates enter active pipelines.

Built for: AI Hiring Managers  •  Enterprise Recruiters  •  Talent Operators
Layer 1

Mandate Intelligence

Turns your JD into a structured role scorecard: must-haves, nice-to-haves, depth expectations, and evaluation checkpoints.

Output: Role scorecard + benchmark rubric
Layer 2 • The Moat

Deployability Scoring

Scores candidates on execution readiness, not resume keywords—using role-aligned benchmarks and proof-of-work validation.

Output: Deployability score + confidence level
Layer 3

Signal Pack Generator

Creates a recruiter-ready signal packet: strengths, gaps, evidence, and recommended interview focus areas.

Output: Signal summary + interview guide
Layer 4

Mandate Matching

Matches candidates to the right roles using signal fit, seniority depth, and domain alignment—so shortlists are actually relevant.

Output: Ranked shortlist + fit rationale
Layer 5

Visibility Dashboard

A recruiter console that shows only validated talent with signal filters, pipeline stage tracking, and decision-ready views.

Output: Signal dashboard + pipeline visibility
